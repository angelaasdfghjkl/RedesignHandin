Redesign

Context

A few summers ago, I chanced upon a new restaurant in my neighborhood called Sushi Village. Eager to check out the menu offerings of the restaurant, I searched for its website on Google. The second the website loaded, I was immediately taken aback by its outdated design and confusing interface. So for this project, I would like to evaluate and redesign the site using usability principles and key design concepts. Browse the original website and see for yourself and let's embark on this journey together.
